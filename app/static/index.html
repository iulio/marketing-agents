Target Location Guide

How to write your target locations:

  • City: Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Timisoara
  • Region: Transylvania, Muntenia, Moldova
  • Country: Romania, Italy, Germany
  • Multiple: Separate with commas: "Bucharest, Cluj, Timisoara"

Common formats:

  • City only: "Bucharest"
  • Country only: "Romania"
  • Radius targeting: "Within 50km of Bucharest"
  • Geo-targeting: "Users in Romania, excluding Bucharest"

Tips:

  • Start with 1-3 cities to test your campaign
  • Use major cities for faster learning
  • Add more locations as you scale
  • Consider time zones for campaign timing
